Cookies

Ingredients

  • Molasses
  • Cinnamon
  • Ginger
  • Cloves
  • Sugar

How To Prepare?

  1. Mix Dry Ingredients: Combine cinnamon, ginger, and cloves in a bowl.
  2. Prepare Dough: Mix molasses with the dry ingredients to form a dough.
  3. Shape Cookies: Roll the dough into balls and coat them with sugar.
  4. Bake: Place the cookies on a baking sheet and bake until edges are slightly crisp.
  5. Cool: Let the cookies cool on a wire rack before serving.

Dal Makhni

Ingredients

  • Whole black lentil (urad dal)
  • Kidney beans
  • Onion-tomato masala
  • Ginger and garlic paste
  • Cream (or coconut/almond milk for a vegan version)
  • Butter or ghee (or vegan butter)
  • Spices: Kasuri methi, paprika, Kashmiri red chili powder
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

How To Prepare?

  1. Soak the lentils and kidney beans overnight.
  2. Cook them in a pressure cooker or slow cooker until soft.
  3. Prepare the masala with onions, tomatoes, ginger, and garlic.
  4. Combine the cooked lentils and beans with the masala.
  5. Simmer on low heat, adding cream and butter.
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve with roti or rice.

Egg Curry

Ingredients

  • 4-5 eggs
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 3-4 Green chilies
  • 4 tbsp Edible Oil
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt as required
  • 1 tsp Red chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 tsp Degi mirch Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam masala powder
  • Handful Chopped Coriander leaves

How To Prepare?

  1. Boil the eggs and peel them carefully, keep them aside.
  2. Heat a pan or kadai and add edible oil.
  3. Chop onions, tomatoes, and green chilies.
  4. Add cumin seeds to the hot oil and cook for about 10 seconds.
  5. Add chopped onions and fry until light brown.
  6. Add ginger-garlic paste and toss gently.
  7. Add chopped tomatoes and green chilies, mix well.
  8. Add salt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder, and Degi mirch powder.
  9. Pour a little water to prevent burning and cook for about 5 minutes.
  10. Add boiled eggs, either whole or halved, and stir gently.
  11. Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  12. Serve hot with Rotis (Indian Bread) or Steamed Rice.
